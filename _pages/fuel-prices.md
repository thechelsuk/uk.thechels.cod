## About this data

Prices are sourced from the [GOV.UK fuel price API](https://www.gov.uk/check-fuel-prices) and updated daily. We track over 100 forecourts within 20 miles of Cheltenham town centre. The table above shows every station that has reported a price change in the last 30 days — stations that haven't filed an update in that window are omitted until they report in.

The As of column shows when each station's prices were last received.

The cheapest prices shown at the top of the page reflect only the stations currently visible in the table — if your local cheapest station hasn't reported recently it may not appear. Always verify prices before making a journey.
